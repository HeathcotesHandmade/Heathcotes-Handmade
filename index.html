About Heathcotes Handmade

Heathcotes Handmade was founded with a passion for creating beautiful, handcrafted items that bring joy to everyday life. Each of our products is thoughtfully designed and carefully made by skilled artisans.

We believe in quality over quantity, sustainable practices, and supporting local craftsmanship. Our materials are ethically sourced, and we strive to minimize waste in all aspects of our production.

What started as a small workshop in our founder's garden shed has grown into a thriving business, but we remain committed to the same values of quality, integrity, and personal touch that inspired us from the beginning.
